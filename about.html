About me

I'm Jacob. I’m a father of two, a passionate cyclist and, a multidisciplinary designer. I like being outdoors, I drink too much coffe, and I love spending time with my family and friends. I’m 30 years old.

From 2010 to 2013 I attended Malmö University and graduated with a bachelor in Interaction Design. During this time I took classes in co-Design and Graphic Design too.

Since 2013 I’ve been working at Hoodin. I'm a founding member and began as Front End Developer. In 2017 I transitioned into my current role as Lead Product Designer.

I’ve also worked on side projects with Youcruit and Kollektiva.

I live and work in Malmö.
