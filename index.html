I’m Jacob, a father of two, an enthusiastic cyclist and a multidisciplinary designer living in Nyköping. I like being outdoors and I love spending time with my family and friends.

From 2010 to 2013 I attended Malmö University and graduated with a bachelor in Interaction Design. During this time I also took classes in Service Design, co-Design, and Graphic Design.

After graduation I joined Hoodin and became a founding member. I wore many hats such as Front End Developer and Product Manager before ending as Lead Product Designer.

Since 2020 I’m a User Experience Designer at design agency Osynlig.
